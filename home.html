Frequently Asked Questions

How does BioSpot work?

BioSpot uses advanced AI technology to analyze uploaded photos and identify plants based on their unique features, such as leaves, flowers, and stems, providing accurate results instantly.

What are the benefits of using BioSpot?

BioSpot provides an easy-to-use platform where you can learn about plants, their habitats, and cultural significance. It makes discovering and understanding plants simple and fun.

You can help by uploading photos of plants, adding to the database, and even donating to support the project. Every little bit makes a big difference.
